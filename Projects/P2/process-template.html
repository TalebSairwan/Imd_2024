What Comes after the environment

Rough Sketches

First Draft

Second draft

I wanted to go for a less formal and pixel perfect format so i decided to move the text to left side of the screen and make the colour of the page all light and tried to make the text the darker green with some texted highlighted in yellow to show the important information in the text.

Final Draft
